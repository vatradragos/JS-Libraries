  • Maria Tănase has a similar importance in Romania as Édith Piaf in France or Amália Rodrigues in Portugal.

  • In 2006 she was included to the list of the 100 Greatest Romanians of all time by a nationwide poll.

  • In 1952, Maria Tănase was offered a position at the Music School No. 1 in Bucharest.

  • "When Maria Tănase sings, beyond her voice, the intelligence feels constantly awake."

    Geo Bogza

  • Nicolae Iorga famously called her "Pasărea măiastră" ("The Majestic Bird").

  • Mihail Sadoveanu considered her "the first authentic rhapsodist" of the Romanians.
